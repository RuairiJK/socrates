Knowledge Management

The primary purpose of this Course is to inform the participant of the principles of Knowledge Management in an organisation and the importance of both sharing and protecting the knowledge as part of an Organisations Intellectual Property.

Topics covered include

  • Knowledge Management Systems and Frameworks
  • Learning Models and their importance to Knowledge Sharing
  • Knowledge Capture and Conversion Tools and Techniques
  • Knowledge Protection, Sharing and Transfer challenges
  • The “5th Discipline” and the Learning Organization
