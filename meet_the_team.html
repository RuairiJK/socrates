Meet the Team

James Kearney

James is an experienced Electronics Engineer who following 16 years in the Medical industry continued to develop his career within the semiconductor industry (Intel) for 15 years gaining significant technical and operational experience. During his career James has held the roles of Design Engineer, Technical Sales Manager , Operations Manager and Technical Training/Education Specialist.

James has been a Technical Trainer/Education specialist for the past 10 years in Intel and has helped develop joint industry/academic partnerships with leading universities and IT colleges.

James delivery style is highly interactive and his experience in facilitating adult learning courses has proven itself time and time again. From problem solving through to vacuum fundamentals, each course is filled with fun and challenging learning, and elicits the unique experience of the participants.

James holds an MSc from Dublin City University in Plasma Physics, a L9 diploma in Lean Systems from University of Limerick and a BSc Hons degree in Electronics from Queens, Belfast.

Paul Comiskey

Paul has 35 years’ experience working as an Engineer, Manager and Lecturer in Military, Marine, Academic and Multinational Organizations.

He has extensive international experience having worked in France, Saudi Arabia, Australia, China and a number of states in North America including Alaska.

Paul has deep knowledge across a wide range of technical disciplines in both Science and Engineering. In the last ten years he has developed and led Cognitive Learning, Analytical Problem Solving and Knowledge Management Programs in leading multinational companies. He is a recognized worldwide expert in these fields.

He is an education pioneer having designed and led the first joint Industry and Academic partnership to deliver Bachelors (BSc) and Masters (MEng) Degrees with two of Irelands leading Universities.

He is an expert communicator and has a unique style of delivery which draws on real world examples and case studies. All his deliveries engage students through practical exercises that challenge their thinking and reinforce learning.

Paul is a qualified Marine Radio Officer and holds a number of qualifications including a Masters Degree in Applied Physics.


Tom Maher

Tom has over 20 years’ experience working as a technician and an engineer across the marine engineering and semiconductor industries. Over the last ten years Tom has been part of a global learning organisation within a leading multinational.

As a Technical Training Engineer Tom has been responsible for an industry leading training lab, designing technical training equipment and programs to be delivered to a highly skilled global workforce. These programs covered a broad technical skill base including Mechanical, Electrical, PLC’s, Microcontrollers, Electronics, Vacuum systems and more. Tom led the standardisation of technical training across sites in Ireland, America, Israel and China. As well as technical training Tom has designed and delivered several team development and leadership training programs, these programs have again been adopted across a global audience. Tom has designed programs that formed part of a joint Industry/University BSc Degree.

Tom led the industry team involved in creating an innovative new QQI Level 6 Manufacturing Technician Skills Program and was responsible for the technical content of the program and was also involved in the design of the training center for the delivery of the program. This was a joint industry/FIT Ltd/ETB program designed to produce highly skilled industry ready technicians and is being delivered by Kildare-Wicklow ETB

Tom has extensive experience delivering training programs, not only to industry but also at University and IT College levels, including online learning. All of Tom’s programs are based on a high level of practical application and are delivered in an engaging and enthusiastic style.

Tom has a BEng in Marine Engineering and a BEng (Hons) in Mechatronics
