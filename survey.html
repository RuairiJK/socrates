Survey

Socrates Workforce Solutions are members of the National Cobotics Steering Committee. This committee is now carrying out a national skills audit within the Cobotics/Robotics fields. The information gathered from this audit will help to shape the future training both from the point of view of which skills are trained and also the way in which they trained.

The audit is being carried out by means of 2 online surveys:

A Training Needs Survey:

This is a short survey that looks at the types of training required by different industries from a high level, the survey takes approximately 2 minutes to complete and can be found at the following link;

Take Training Needs Survey

A Skills Needs Survey:

The survey takes a more in-depth look at the specific skills needs of industry in regard to Cobotics and Robotics. The survey is designed to build a detailed map of not only the skills required by each industry sector but also the availability of those skills.
The survey can be found at the following link;

Take Skills Needs Survey

The accuracy of this national audit and the direction of the future provision of these skills depends on having as complete a picture as possible so we would therefore encourage you to take a few minutes to complete the surveys. Please feel free to also share this audit information with your colleagues within industry.

These surveys are being carried out anonymously and behalf of the National Cobotics Steering Committee.
